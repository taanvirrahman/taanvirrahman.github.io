# Design in the Age of AI

As we move further into 2026, the intersection of AI and design has become more than just a trend. It's a fundamental shift in how we create.

## The Human Element

While AI can generate thousands of variations in seconds, the role of the designer has shifted from "creator" to "curator" and "director". We provide the emotional context that AI still lacks.

- **Emotional Resonance**: AI doesn't feel, but humans do.
- **Strategic Thinking**: Design is about solving problems, not just making things look pretty.
